Why It Matters

Plant diseases can significantly impact crop yields and the overall health of gardens. Early detection is crucial in mitigating damage and ensuring sustainable agriculture. Our system empowers users to take charge of their plant care, reducing reliance on chemical treatments and promoting environmentally friendly practices.
